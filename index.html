Matram Seva Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to the betterment of society. It was founded by Vishekha Nand Vishu. The foundation aims to promote a healthy future for women and girls. Although it was officially established in 2024, it has been actively working since 2021 to uplift women. The organization runs several campaigns, such as

  • #DonatePadBeGlad
  • #1stPeriodCelebrations
  • #LetsTalkAboutPeriods
  • #ChuppiTodoYatra

to raise awareness about the importance of menstrual health and hygiene. It also provides free sanitary pads to underprivileged women and girls in society. The foundation has been recognized and awarded by the late Shri Sushil Kumar Modi. Since 2021, it has also been a part of the Global Revolutionary Educational Society.
